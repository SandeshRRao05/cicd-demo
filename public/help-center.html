Getting Started

Welcome to AWS Cloud Academy

This platform is designed to help you learn AWS cloud services and prepare for AWS certifications. Here's how to get started:

  1. Explore Courses - Browse through our catalog of AWS courses
  2. Join a Learning Path - Follow structured learning paths for specific AWS certifications
  3. Practice Labs - Get hands-on experience with AWS services in our labs
  4. Take Assessments - Test your knowledge with quizzes and practice exams

Pro Tip

Start with the AWS Cloud Practitioner course if you're new to AWS, then progress to more specialized certifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked in your dashboard. You can see completion percentages for each course and your overall certification path.

Can I download course materials for offline use?

Yes, most course materials are available for download as PDFs. Look for the download icon next to each lesson.

How do I get my AWS certification after completing a course?

Our courses prepare you for AWS certification exams, but you'll need to schedule and take the official AWS exam separately. We provide links to the AWS Certification portal in each course.
